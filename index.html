Tong Lab

A small but ambitious lab for AI research, multimodal learning, agent systems, and the computational workflows that make research life actually work.

Tong Lab centers on Tong Zhang's evolving work across AI, multimodal understanding, world models, safety, and research tooling. The lab is also an experiment in a new working style: combining one human researcher with a small stack of specialized assistants that help with thinking, execution, infrastructure, and day-to-day operations.

Right now that stack includes Rock for research support and coordination, Kiti for VPS / Ubuntu-side infrastructure and long-running services, and Biubiu for Mac-side coding and browser workflows.
