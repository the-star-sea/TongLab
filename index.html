Tong Lab

Research, systems, and living AI workflows.

Tong Lab is a small research-and-building space around Tong Zhang's work in AI, multimodal learning, agent systems, and computational tools for research life. This site is the public front door for the lab's people, projects, notes, and ways to connect.

Right now the lab includes Tong and three assistant operators: Rock for coordination, research support, and Windows / WSL-side operations; Kiti for VPS / Ubuntu infrastructure and long-running services; and Biubiu for Mac-side coding and browser workflows.
