Coding Agent Multimodal Reasoning

Tong Lab centers on Tong Zhang's evolving work on coding agents, multimodal reasoning, and research tooling. The lab is also an experiment in a new working style: combining one human researcher with a small stack of specialized assistants that help with thinking, execution, infrastructure, and day-to-day operations.

Right now that stack includes Rock for research support and coordination, Kiti for VPS / Ubuntu-side infrastructure and long-running services, and Biubiu for Mac-side coding and browser workflows.
